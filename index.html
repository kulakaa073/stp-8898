About this game

Welcome to Marbelicious, the ultimate marble shooter experience where relaxation meets colorful fun! With over 1000 lovingly designed levels, you'll blast marbles, rescue dragons, and craft new worlds. Whether you have five minutes or fifty, dive into a soothing puzzle escape full of joyful popping sounds and magical visuals.

Game Features

  • 1000+ Relaxing Levels - Enjoy endless marble-blasting fun, stress-free.

  • Rescue Dragons - Save adorable dragons and unlock magical helpers.

  • Match-3 Mechanics - Easy to learn, rewarding to master.

  • Vibrant Art & Design - A burst of color with every level.

  • Boosters & Power-ups - Including the mighty DRACARYS!

  • World-Building Quests - Help dragons rebuild their enchanted lands.

  • Soothing Soundscape - Immersive sounds for a peaceful experience.

How to Play

  • Tap to aim and shoot colorful marbles.

  • Match 3 or more to make them pop!

  • Swap marbles by tapping the dragon shooter.

  • Use boosters to clear tricky levels.

  • Rescue dragons, collect rewards, and rebuild magical lands.
